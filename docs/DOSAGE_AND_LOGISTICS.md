# 05_DOSAGE_AND_LOGISTICS.md

## Investigational Pharmacy Dispensing Instructions

This document provides the standard calculations required for Section 5 (Logistics and Supply) of your Single-Patient Expanded Access application.

### 1. Verification of Formulations
Hospitals must specify which preparation type is being requested under the current IND application when finalizing the procurement invoice.

*   **Tablet Regimen:** High-purity oil core tablet, pre-measured at 140mg per unit.
*   **Liquid Regimen:** Bulk fluid solution measured in standard fluid ounces, dispensed with calibrated medical syringes or cups.

### 2. Protocol Duration and Shipping Adjustments
Per FDA compliance protocols, a 10% waste and damage buffer is calculated automatically into the manufacturer's final clinical training shipment. This ensures that accidental fluid spillage or broken tablets do not compromise patient adherence across the 6-month timeline.

### 3. Shipping & Verification Data (.tsv format)
For automated ingestion into the hospital's Electronic Health Record (EHR) inventory system, import the raw data from `supply_matrix.tsv` located in this repository's root folder.
